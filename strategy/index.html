    Business Strategy — Robot Fantôme Ventures¶

    "A mechanic who codes, builds AI agents, and plays punk rock walks into a market gap..."

    Created: 2026-03-01 | Review cycle: Monthly

    Business Structure¶

    Robot Fantôme (Absolutely Plausible) is OUR business — the umbrella brand for everything we build, sell, and create.

    hackFatura is a CLIENT PROJECT — built for Parazinho Chassis Jig (PCJ), owned by Thiago Pereira. It is NOT our brand.

    Robot Fantôme (Us) Client Projects (hackFatura, etc.)
    Brand Clean, white, editorial, minimalist Client's own
    Domain robotfantome.com mech.robotfantome.com (subdomain)
    Revenue Consulting, content, SaaS, products Client deliverable → portfolio + case study
    Music Infused in OUR content only Not in client deliverables

    The Unfair Advantage¶

    • 19 years in niche motorsport mechanics (karting chassis — since 2007)
    • Builder — ships real software (hackFatura: zero-cost field app, live in a single session)
    • AI-native — runs own agent infrastructure, understands automation deeply
    • 100% OSS + sustainability values — brand differentiator, not a constraint
    • Original music — robot fantôme tracks in ALL content (unique sonic brand, no licensing costs)

    PLAN A: hackFatura → SaaS¶

    Quick Win (Week 1-2): Sell Custom Builds¶

    Take the hackFatura architecture (not PCJ's branding), build a custom-branded version for other mechanics:

    • Their branding, their colors, their Google Sheet + Apps Script
    • Deploy to their GitHub Pages
    • Price: $300-500 per setup (~2-3 hours with AI assistance)
    • Target: mechanics you already know from the karting circuit

    Revenue Model (Month 3+)¶

    Tier Price Features
    Starter $9/mo Single user, 100 invoices/mo, basic dashboard
    Pro $29/mo Multi-user (up to 5), unlimited invoices, custom branding, full PDF export
    Team $59/mo Unlimited users, multi-location, analytics, API access

    Revenue Projection¶

    Month Custom Builds SaaS MRR
    1 $300-1,000 $0
    3 $400-1,200 $100-300
    6 on-demand $300-600

    PLAN B: AI Automation Consulting¶

    Quick Win (Week 1): AI Audits¶

    • 30-minute call → identify 3-5 automation opportunities → 1-page report
    • Price: $150 (or free if they commit to a project within 30 days)
    • Time to deliver: 1 hour total

    Service Offerings¶

    Service Price Deliverable
    AI Audit $150 30-min call + written report
    Workflow Build $500-2,000 Custom automation (Sheets + Apps Script)
    Zero-Cost MVP $2,000-5,000 Full app like hackFatura
    Monthly Retainer $500-1,000/mo Ongoing maintenance + new automations

    Positioning¶

    Anti-agency. Anti-bloat. Anti-vendor-lock-in.

    "I build real business tools for $0/month. No subscriptions. No hosting fees. You own everything."

    PLAN C: Content + Education¶

    Quick Win (Week 2): Gumroad Digital Product¶

    "Zero-Cost Business App Blueprint" — $19-29 - Architecture template, Apps Script starter, GitHub Pages guide, jsPDF invoice template

    Content Pillars¶

    1. "Zero-Cost Tech" — Build real tools for $0/month
    2. "AI for Real Work" — Practical automation, not hype
    3. "Mechanic Who Codes" — The personal brand story
    4. "OSS & Sustainability" — Open-source everything

    Music as a Content Weapon¶

    robot fantôme music in everything: YouTube intros/outros, tutorial backgrounds, product launches. No licensing costs. No copyright strikes. A moat no other tech creator has.

    Revenue Timeline¶

    When What Revenue
    Week 2 Gumroad blueprint $19-29/sale
    Month 3 hackFatura landing + signup $100-300/mo
    Month 5 Course pre-sale ($99-149) $1,000-3,000 (launch)
    Month 6 Newsletter sponsorships $200-400/issue

         Content + Music (Plan C)

          ↓            ↓

          ↘         ↙
       Case Studies + Revenue
          ↓
       More Content → repeat

    Core Principles (Non-Negotiable)¶

    • $0 infrastructure until revenue justifies spend
    • Open-source first — always
    • Zero-waste stack — minimal cloud footprint
    • Build in public — transparency is the brand
    • Original music everywhere — robot fantôme is the soundtrack
    • Mechanic work stays until online revenue replaces it
    • Charge early, charge often
