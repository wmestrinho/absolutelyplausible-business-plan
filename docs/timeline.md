# 6-Month Execution Timeline — Robot Fantôme Ventures

**Start date:** 2026-03-01
**North star:** $3,000-5,000/mo online income by September 2026
**Hard constraint:** Mechanic work continues. All online work fits in 10-15 hours/week.
**Review cycle:** Weekly (Sunday) + Monthly (first Sunday)

---

## Planning Principles

1. **Max 5 tasks per week.** Not 11. Not 8. Five. If you finish early, pull from next week.
2. **Revenue tasks first.** If you only have 2 hours this week, spend them on something that makes money.
3. **Batch creative work.** Write 2 weeks of LinkedIn posts in one sitting. Record 2 videos in one day.
4. **Track hours.** Know where your 10-15 hours actually go. Adjust monthly.
5. **Travel weeks = maintenance mode.** On the road for mechanic work? Do outreach and follow-ups. Don't try to build.
